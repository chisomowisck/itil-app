### Starting an Exam
1. Click "Mock Exam" from the home page
2. Review exam information (40 questions, 60 minutes)
3. Click "Start Exam"

### During the Exam
1. **Read the question** - Category badge shows the topic
2. **Select an answer** - Click A, B, C, or D
3. **Flag for review** - Click "Flag" button if unsure
4. **Mark important** - Click "Mark" button with star icon
5. **Toggle score** - Click eye icon to show/hide current score
6. **Navigate** - Use Previous/Next buttons or sidebar
7. **Submit** - Click green "Submit Exam" button on last question

### After the Exam
1. **View results** - See your score, pass/fail status, and time spent
2. **Review questions** - See all questions with correct answers
3. **Check explanations** - Understand why answers are correct

### Tracking Progress

## ðŸ’¡ Tips for Best Results

### Using the Score Toggle
- **Hide score** during practice to simulate real exam conditions
- **Show score** when learning to track progress in real-time
- Score only counts answered questions (not unanswered)

### Flagging Strategy
- **Flag** questions you're unsure about
- Use "First Flagged" button to review them later
- Filter by "Flagged" in progress to revisit difficult questions

### Important Questions
- **Mark** questions that teach key concepts
- Use "Important" filter to create a custom study set
- Review these before taking the real exam

### Sidebar Navigation
- **All (40)** - See all questions
- **Flagged** - Only flagged questions
- **Important** - Only important questions
- **Done** - Only answered questions
- Click any number to jump to that question

### Help Panel
- **Tips** - Exam strategies and time management
- **Rules** - Exam format and passing criteria
- **Review** - Quick lists of unanswered/flagged/important
- **Hide panel** - Click X to get more screen space

## ðŸŽ“ Study Recommendations

### First Time Users
1. Take a practice exam with score **hidden**
2. Review results and note weak categories
3. Study those categories in Study Guide
4. Retake exam with score **visible** to track improvement

### Regular Practice
1. Take exams regularly (e.g., weekly)
2. Flag difficult questions
3. Review flagged questions before next exam
4. Track improvement trend

### Before Real Exam
1. Review all "Important" questions
2. Check score trend - aim for consistent 70%+
3. Review categories where you score lowest
4. Take final practice exam in real conditions (score hidden, 60 min timer)
