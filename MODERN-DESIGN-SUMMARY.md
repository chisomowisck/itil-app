## ðŸ”„ Border Radius System

- **rounded-full**: Pills, badges (9999px)
- **rounded-2xl**: Hero cards, major sections (1rem)

- **rounded-lg**: Buttons, inputs, small cards (0.5rem)
- **rounded**: Minimal rounding (0.25rem)

## ðŸ’« Shadow System

- **shadow-sm**: Subtle elevation (header, small cards)
- **shadow**: Default card shadow
- **shadow-lg**: Important elements (CTA buttons, submit)
- **shadow-xl**: Major cards (results, hero)
- **hover:shadow-lg**: Interactive cards
